** Definitions

What is DevOps?
DevOps is a methodology which brings development and opreation team together to make process faster and reliale.

What is Agile?
The agile methodology is a project management approach that involves breaking the project into phases and emphasize
continuous collabration and improvement.
Teams follow a cycle of planning, executing and evaluating.

Flow of agile:
MEET --> PLAN --> DESIGN --> DEVELOP --> TEST --> EVALUATE

What is Jenkins?
Jenkins is an open source continuous integration/continuous delivery and development CI/CD automation software.
It is used to implement the CI/CD workflows, called pipeline.
